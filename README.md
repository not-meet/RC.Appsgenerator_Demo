# DEMO SHOWED IN THE RC CHANNEL! 
      
## The Prompt

A completely non-technical user types:

```
make my team more productive
```

That's it. No technical details. No framework knowledge. Just an idea.

---

## Step 1 — Gemini Asks a Simple Question

Rather than making assumptions, Gemini asks one plain English question to understand the intent better.

<img width="1920" height="836" alt="march-18" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/533236b2-c32b-4edf-a783-02715bc24258" />

---

## Step 2 — Gemini Presents a Plan

Taking the user's answer into account along with the feature keyword map in `GEMINI.md`, Gemini figures out what kind of app to build, lays out the plan in plain language, and asks for a green flag or any changes before writing a single line of code.

<img width="1920" height="836" alt="march-18" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/02daf512-15a6-4741-9b77-8c51b11239a3" />

---

## Step 3 — Gemini Loads Only the Relevant Skills

Once approved, Gemini picks only the skill files needed for this specific app. Everything else stays unloaded.

<img width="1915" height="499" alt="mar-18-2" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/52c174f6-6028-4920-8541-471c4908f6a7" />

---

## Step 4 — App is Built, Reviewed, and Packaged

Gemini generates the full app, runs the review skill to catch any silent bugs, and packages everything into a ready-to-submit zip.

https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9ae03b37-cb2e-4211-9a6f-b4d1230fef13

---

That's the whole flow. One prompt. One working Rocket.Chat App.
